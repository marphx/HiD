▣ INTERFACE COLOR SCHEME
▣ KEYBOARD MAP & USAGE
PIANO: Z–M+,./ = lower whites · S D G H J L ; = lower blacks · Q–] = upper whites · 2 3 5 6 7 9 0 = upper blacks

CHORD SLOTS: 1–0 trigger slots 1–10 · ,. with SHIFT cycles slots in order

GLOBAL: SPACE sustain · TAB chromatic · L loop · K stop loop · T tap · [] oct · −= trans

SAMPLER: F1–F8 trigger pads 1–8 · pads 9–16 are configurable

WINDOWS: Click launcher buttons above piano to toggle windows. Drag titles to move. Drag bottom-right corner to resize. Last-clicked window comes to front.

BUILD MODE: When ON, clicking a piano key just toggles its illumination (the "shape") without playing. PLAY LIT triggers all illuminated keys as a chord. With SUSTAIN ON, the chord rings until you trigger the next chord — voice-stealing crossfade prevents clicks.

CHORD CHANGES: Switching chord slots while sustaining cleanly fades the old chord out (~28ms) and fades the new one in. No overlap, no clash.

KILL: Cuts all audio immediately and resets running voices, loop, sampler.
